ENTJ

( Extraverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Judging )

Commander

About

  • ENTJs are known for their strong leadership skills, strategic thinking, and ability to organize and execute plans effectively.
  • They are often seen as natural leaders who are confident, decisive, and assertive.
  • ENTJs are known for their ability to think strategically and make sound decisions, often leading by example and inspiring others.
  • They are often seen as ambitious and driven, with a strong desire to achieve their goals and make a positive impact on the world.
  • ENTJs are often seen as confident and assertive, with a strong sense of self and a clear vision for their future.

Strengths

  • Strong leadership skills
  • Strategic thinking and planning abilities
  • Ability to organize and execute plans effectively
  • Confidence, decisiveness, and assertiveness
  • Ability to think strategically and make sound decisions
  • Ambitious and driven
  • Strong sense of self and clear vision for their future

Weaknesses

  • May be overly concerned with intellectual pursuits and may struggle to engage in social interactions
