INFP
Mediator





About

INFPs are idealistic, creative, and introspective individuals who value authenticity and personal growth. They are often seen as dreamers and are driven by their values and beliefs. INFPs are empathetic and compassionate, often seeking to understand the emotions of others. They may struggle with practical matters and can be sensitive to criticism.



Strengths

  • Creative
  • Empathy
  • Have strong values
  • Able to see the world in a unique way
  • Can inspire others with their ideas
  • Connect with others on an emotional level, make them a great friend and partner


Weaknesses

  • Struggle with practical matters
  • Sensitive to criticism
  • May struggle with social interaction
  • May be more introverted than extroverted


Relationships

INFPs are often drawn to partners who share their values and beliefs. INFPs are often seen as loyal and supportive partners, but they may also need space and time to process their emotions.



Career

INFPs are often drawn to careers that allow them to express their creativity and values. They may be drawn to careers in the arts, writing, counseling, or social work.
